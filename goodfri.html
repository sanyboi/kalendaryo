GOOD FRIDAY


Good Friday is the Christian holy day that commemorates the crucifixion of Jesus Christ and his death at Calvary. It falls on the Friday before Easter Sunday and is part of the Holy Week observances. Good Friday is a solemn day of reflection and mourning for many Christians, as they contemplate the sacrifice of Jesus for the forgiveness of sins. It is typically marked by church services, scripture readings, prayers, and devotional reflections on the suffering and death of Jesus.
