BONIFACIO DAY


Bonifacio Day is a national holiday in the Philippines observed on November 30th each year. It commemorates the birth anniversary of Andrés Bonifacio, one of the most prominent figures in the Philippine Revolution against Spanish colonial rule.

Andrés Bonifacio was a key leader of the Katipunan, a revolutionary society that aimed to achieve Philippine independence through armed struggle. He played a crucial role in organizing and mobilizing Filipinos to fight against Spanish oppression and tyranny.

Bonifacio Day is a time for Filipinos to remember and honor Andrés Bonifacio's contributions to the struggle for Philippine independence. It serves as a reminder of his courage, leadership, and dedication to the cause of freedom.
