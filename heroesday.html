NATIONAL HEROES DAY


National Heroes Day in the Philippines is a national holiday observed on the last Monday of August each year. It honors and pays tribute to the country's national heroes and martyrs who played significant roles in the struggle for Philippine independence and freedom.

The holiday commemorates the heroism and sacrifices of notable figures in Philippine history, including Andres Bonifacio, Jose Rizal, Emilio Aguinaldo, Apolinario Mabini, and other revolutionary leaders, as well as lesser-known individuals who fought against colonial oppression and foreign rule.
