LABOR DAY


Labor Day in the Philippines, also known as "Araw ng mga Manggagawa" or "Araw ng Paggawa," is a national holiday celebrated on May 1st each year. It recognizes the contributions and achievements of Filipino workers and laborers. Labor Day is an occasion to honor the labor movement and the struggles of workers for better working conditions, fair wages, and labor rights.

Labor Day serves as a reminder of the significance of labor in nation-building and economic development. It is also a time to reflect on the challenges facing Filipino workers and to renew commitments to uphold their rights and welfare. Additionally, it provides an opportunity for workers to celebrate their solidarity and unity in pursuing social justice and equity in the workplace.
